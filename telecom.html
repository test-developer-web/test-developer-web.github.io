2G/3G/LTE/5G Rollout

Seamless, high-performance mobile network deployments for operators worldwide.

At Vertex One Innovations, we specialize in the end-to-end deployment of 2G, 3G, LTE, and 5G networks, ensuring seamless integration and high-performance connectivity for mobile operators worldwide. Whether deploying macro sites, small cells, or Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS), we provide operators with cutting-edge solutions tailored to urban, suburban, and rural environments.

1. UMTS/WCDMA/LTE Extensions

Enhancing network capacity, coverage, and seamless integration with existing infrastructure.

  • Spectrum refarming and carrier aggregation for efficient bandwidth utilization.
  • Network densification strategies to enhance coverage and user experience.
  • Parameter tuning for optimal spectral efficiency and minimized service disruption.

2. Network Optimization

Ensuring superior call quality, data speeds, and overall network performance.

  • AI-driven RF tuning, interference mitigation, and load balancing.
  • Real-time network performance monitoring and predictive modeling.
  • Handover improvements and congestion management strategies.

3. 4G Network Drivetest and Analysis

Ensuring high-quality, seamless connectivity through real-world network testing.

  • KPI-based performance assessment for coverage gaps and throughput issues.
  • Advanced post-processing software for latency analysis and handover failures.
  • VoLTE optimization, in-building coverage improvements, and dropped call reduction.

4. 5G Optimization

Advanced tuning for ultra-reliable low-latency communications, IoT connectivity, and broadband services.

  • Beamforming strategies and massive MIMO optimization.
  • Dynamic spectrum sharing (DSS) and mobility management enhancement.
  • Network slicing, handover performance, and power control tuning.

5. BSC/RNC/MSC/MGW Deployment

Ensuring a highly available, scalable, and resilient telecom network.

  • Planning, hardware installation, and software configuration of core network elements.
  • Interoperability testing and performance verification.
  • Optimized traffic management and enhanced signaling capacity.
